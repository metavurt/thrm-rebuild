Employment Opportunities

Technicians

Assembly and Test Technician

Description

Thermco Instrument Corporation is seeking an assembly and test technician to work at Thermco’s headquarters and manufacturing plant located in La Porte, Indiana.

General Responsibilites

The ability to use hand tools, read and understand basic schematics and diagrams, and work in a detailed and organized fashion. Experience in assembly and testing is beneficial, but Thermco will train the qualified individual to build and test our particular product. Teamwork and a strong work ethic are essential.

Qualifications

High School Degree

Must be able to read blue prints, schematics, wiring diagrams, piping diagrams, etc.

Must have experience with basic hand tools.
